WHY Web Development???

Imagine that you're lying on a beach off of the Adriatic Coast. It's sunny outside, the water is blue, the sky is cloudless, and you're holding a margarita, extra tequila. You think life couldn't get better, when all of a sudden a beautiful girl saunters by holding a water bottle, glossy hair whipping in the wind and sunglasses masking what you just know are eyes that will pierce you right to your bones. Just as she's passing your spot, she drops her water bottle onto your lap and it sprays everywhere. You're delighted, because now you have an excuse to talk to her. She apologizes profusely for spilling on you and tells you her name (Something Exotic), offering a handshake. For the next ten minutes you laugh together, and you're certain you've found The One. Suddenly, her friend calls her from down the beach and tells her to hurry up, it's time to go.

"Wait!" you exclaim as she turns to leave. "Can I get your number?"

"Oh," she smiles sadly. "I'm sorry, but I don't have one." And with that, she's gone. You never see her again. She's on your mind for the next week, but you can't find her anywhere and there's no way to get in touch. You give up, life goes on, and eventually you meet another beautiful girl.

When you run a business or sell a product, it is unequivocally paramount that the quality of what you're selling is top-notch. But that's only half the battle. Without an online presence, your product becomes that beautiful girl on the beach who can't be reached again and is eventually forgotten about, no matter how tempting she once was. You can run the most captivating, useful, ingenious business, but if there's no way to find it, it will eventually be substituted with something else. Had the girl on the beach only had a phone number, you might have entered into a lifelong relationship with her. By the same token, if your business has a web presence, you create the potential to have customers for life.

A website development tool provides companies with a podium for this necessary web presence and allows them to get in touch with millions of web surfers all over the world, widening the range of their potential customers. This doesn't just make the companies accessible, but accessible to the global population. It also gives businesses the chance to portray themselves in the ways they want to be seen, with a platform that's built on their terms and can effectively market the services or products that they offer. In addition, a website will make businesses look more reliable if they're represented in a professional way. To develop a website in accordance with specific criteria, the business can either hire a developer or do it themselves by choosing from the best web development software, which will assist in creating polished websites.

Website development can include anything from simply programming the data of a website to adding client liaison, content, network security and web server configuration, e-commerce development and more. It usually ranges from creating the simplest, plain text to the most difficult web-based applications, social network services and electronic business. Some website developers incorporate design into their web building, while others leave it strictly at coding and writing mark-up. It should go without saying that the better a website looks, the more visitors it will attract, so it's worthwhile to focus on the design aspects as well as the coding when developing your website. The content should accurately depict what your service provides and be geared towards enticing the site's visitors, in order to keep them engaged.

If you want your business to flourish, web development is crucial. Developing a well-designed, professional looking website will get your foot in the door of the online market, and give you a base from which to build your clientele. You may have lost the girl on the beach, but it’s not too late for your company. Get online, build a website, and prepare to keep your customers forever.
